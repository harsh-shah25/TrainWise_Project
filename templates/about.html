About Trainwise

Trainwise is a revolutionary web application that helps users monitor their exercise posture in real-time. Our advanced technology uses computer vision to analyze the user's movements and provide instant feedback on whether they are performing exercises correctly or not.

With Trainwise, you can ensure that you are getting the most out of your workout routine while minimizing the risk of injury. Whether you're a beginner or a fitness enthusiast, Trainwise is your ultimate fitness companion.

Our mission is to empower individuals to achieve their fitness goals safely and effectively. We believe that everyone deserves access to high-quality fitness guidance, and Trainwise is here to make that a reality.
